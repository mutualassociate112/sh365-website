About Smart Healthcare 365

A trusted partner in healthcare staffing, committed to quality, compliance, and the highest standards of patient care.

Our Mission

Smart Healthcare 365 exists to bridge the gap between healthcare facilities that need qualified staff and healthcare professionals seeking meaningful work. We are committed to delivering credentialed, dependable personnel who uphold the highest standards of patient care and clinical safety.

As a New York State Department of Health registered Temporary Health Care Services Agency (THCSA), we operate under the strict requirements of Article 29-K of the Public Health Law. This means every professional we place has been rigorously screened, verified, and credentialed before their first shift at your facility.

Our Values

Partnership Approach

Responsiveness

Compliance Commitment

As a registered THCSA under Article 29-K, we maintain full compliance with the New York State Department of Health's staffing agency regulations, including:

  • Comprehensive credentialing and background check requirements
  • Mandatory workers' compensation and professional liability insurance
  • Prohibition of non-compete clauses for healthcare workers
  • Record retention for a minimum of 6 years per DOH requirements
  • HIPAA-compliant information handling and data security
  • Full compliance with NYS labor laws including paid sick leave

Our 8-Step Credentialing Process

Every healthcare professional we place undergoes our rigorous verification process to ensure patient safety and regulatory compliance.

1

Application Review

Thorough review of employment history, qualifications, and career objectives.

2

License Verification

Primary source verification through the NYS Education Department for all professional licenses.

3

Certification Verification

Verification of CNA certification through the NYS Nurse Aide Registry and other applicable registries.

4

Education Verification

Confirmation of nursing degrees, training programs, and specialized certifications.

5

Background Checks

Criminal background check, OIG exclusion list, SAM.gov, and sex offender registry screening.

6

Employment & References

Verification of past employment and professional reference checks from supervisors and colleagues.

7

Health Screening

TB testing, drug screening, immunization records, and current BLS/CPR certification verification.

8

Continuing Education

Verification of required continuing education credits and ongoing professional development.

All credentialing records are maintained for a minimum of 6 years per NYS DOH requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from facilities and healthcare workers about our staffing services.

For Healthcare Facilities

A Temporary Health Care Services Agency (THCSA) is an organization registered with the New York State Department of Health under Article 29-K of the Public Health Law. THCSAs are authorized to provide temporary healthcare staffing to healthcare facilities across the state. Registration requires compliance with strict credentialing, insurance, and operational standards established by the DOH.

Yes. Smart Healthcare 365 is a registered Temporary Health Care Services Agency (THCSA) with the New York State Department of Health under Article 29-K of the Public Health Law. We maintain all required registrations, insurance coverage, and operational standards mandated by the DOH.

Every healthcare professional we place has completed our comprehensive 8-step credentialing process. This includes primary source license verification, certification checks, education verification, criminal background checks, OIG and SAM.gov exclusion screening, sex offender registry checks, employment and reference verification, health screening (TB, drug screen, immunizations, BLS/CPR), and continuing education verification. All credentials are maintained and monitored on an ongoing basis.

We offer same-day staffing for many requests. For per diem shifts, we can often confirm coverage within 2 to 4 hours of your request. For contract and specialized positions, we typically present qualified candidates within 24 to 48 hours. Our 24/7 on-call team ensures you can reach us anytime a staffing need arises.

Our rates are competitive and transparent. Bill rates vary based on the position type, specialty, shift timing, and assignment duration. We provide clear rate sheets with no hidden fees. Contact us for a customized rate quote based on your specific staffing requirements.

Yes. We maintain comprehensive insurance coverage including general liability, professional liability (malpractice), and workers' compensation insurance as required by Article 29-K. Certificates of insurance are available upon request.

We offer flexible engagement terms. There are no long-term contracts required for per diem staffing. Contract assignments have defined terms based on the placement duration. We do not impose volume minimums, so you can use our services as often or as little as your needs dictate.

Yes. Many of our temporary placements lead to permanent hires. We are happy to discuss conversion arrangements. Per Article 29-K, our healthcare workers are not subject to non-compete clauses, so they are free to accept direct employment offers from facilities where they have been placed.
